# Audio Samples

Place your intro and outro audio files here:

- `intro.mp3` — Played before the narration. Recommended: 5–15 seconds of calm, ambient music that fades in.
- `outro.mp3` — Played after the narration. Recommended: 5–15 seconds that fades out gently.

## Tips for "Goodnight Read" Style

- Use soft, ambient music (lo-fi, classical piano, nature sounds)
- Keep intro/outro short so the narration remains the focus
- Normalize your audio files to -14 LUFS for consistency
- Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC
